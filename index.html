ABOUT THE GAME

HISTORY

Once upon a time horror came to the city - it was enslaved by terrifying creatures. They come up from their tombs when the moon is full destroying everything thay can find. Nothing was able to kill them, even the bravest warriors was defeted by this monsters. Desperation embraced the city. After years of fear they ask you for help. Citizens gave you a magic gun - it is able to kill this zombies.
Good luck, warrior, you are only hope!

HOW TO PLAY

The goal of this game - to kill as many zombies as possible. You can shoot them with your gun or jump over their heads. The more monsters are killed, the faster thay run. Red bats are your enemies too, try to avoid facing them. Staying on the one place or moving forward is a matter of preference, but to experience more hard mode game we strongly recommend you to run.
Top Tip:
Don't forget to enjoy the game (:

TECHNOLOGY

The game was created using vanilla Javascript, HTML5 and CSS3. We didn't use any frameworks while creating Zombie Plantation

FULL SCREEN

To get fulfilled gamer experience we recommend to use full Screen mode

SUPPORT

All the technologies, used in this game, supported by modern browsers. Unfortunately at this very moment our game doesn't work properly on the devices with the width of the screen less than 1280 pixels, but we're working on it(no we don't but we might will)
